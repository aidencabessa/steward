The problem
The law changes.
Most landlords don't notice.

Landlord-tenant law is one of the most actively legislated areas in the country. New rent control ordinances, disclosure requirements, and eviction rules pass every year — often at the city level, not just state.

Most small landlords find out they've made a legal mistake when a tenant sues them. By then it's too late and a lawyer costs $300/hour.

Steward Weekly monitors every change and explains what it means for you — in plain English, every week, free.
